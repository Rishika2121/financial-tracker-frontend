Tax Deduction Eligibility & Provision Engine

A deterministic, rule-based tax intelligence engine that evaluates deduction eligibility using verified legal logic, structured inputs, and compliance-first safeguards.

How the Engine Operates

Each claimed deduction passes through a multi-layer validation pipeline before being included in tax preparation.

  • Country-specific statutory rule book
  • User identity & profile classification
  • Income stream & source mapping
  • Document and proof validation

Validation & Rule Enforcement

  • Section eligibility (80C, 80D, 24(b), 44AD)
  • Maximum deduction thresholds
  • Inter-section dependency checks
  • Old vs New regime compatibility
  • Disallowance & exclusion rules

Eligibility Classification

Eligible

Meets all statutory conditions and is safely claimable without additional intervention.

Conditionally Eligible

Requires fulfillment of additional limits, documentation, or regime-specific conditions.

Not Eligible

Disallowed under applicable tax provisions and excluded from computation.

Decision Flow Overview

  • Data ingestion & normalization
  • Expense categorization
  • Section mapping
  • Rule & limit evaluation
  • Eligibility classification
  • Explainable result generation

Compliance & Risk Mitigation

The engine proactively prevents over-claiming, duplicate deductions, and non-compliant entries by enforcing statutory limits and exclusion rules at every stage.
