Key Tax Deductions

Section 80C – Core Investment Deduction

Maximum Combined Limit: ₹1,50,000

  • Life Insurance Premium
  • Tuition Fees (Max 2 children)
  • Home Loan Principal Repayment
  • PPF / EPF / ELSS / 5-Year FD
Section 24(b) – Home Loan Interest

Up to ₹2,00,000 (Self-Occupied)

  • Interest paid on housing loan
  • Separate from 80C principal
Section 80GG – Rent Paid (No HRA)
  • Claim deduction if HRA not received
  • Available for salaried & self-employed
  • Subject to prescribed limits
Section 80D – Medical Insurance
  • ₹25,000 (Self + Family)
  • Additional ₹25,000 for parents
  • ₹50,000 if senior citizen
Section 80E – Higher Education Loan
  • No Maximum Limit
  • Interest portion only
  • Available for 8 years
Section 80TTA / 80TTB – Savings Interest
  • ₹10,000 (Below 60 years)
  • ₹50,000 (Senior Citizens)
Section 80CCD(1B) – Extra NPS Benefit
  • Additional ₹50,000
  • Over & above ₹1.5L (80C)
  • Applies to National Pension Scheme
