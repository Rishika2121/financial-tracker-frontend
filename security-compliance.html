Security & Compliance Framework

A multi-layered protection and governance framework designed to safeguard sensitive financial data, enforce regulatory compliance, and ensure audit readiness at every stage.

End-to-End Data Protection

Shunyatax secures all sensitive information using industry-standard cryptographic controls throughout its lifecycle.

  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Encryption of data in transit
  • Secure storage for uploaded documents
  • Strict access-based data visibility

Country & Jurisdiction Compliance

Tax logic and validations are enforced strictly according to the user’s country and applicable jurisdiction.

  • Country-specific tax rule enforcement
  • Jurisdiction-aware validations
  • Local regulatory alignment
  • Automated compliance enforcement

Audit Trail & Traceability

Comprehensive Activity Logging

All sensitive actions—uploads, edits, validations, approvals, and overrides—are logged with timestamps.

Full Traceability

Every change is traceable to its source, enabling accountability and audit transparency.

Controlled Tax Rule Governance

  • Tax rules maintained only by authorized administrators
  • Automatic updates when tax laws change
  • Version-controlled rule modifications
  • No end-user manipulation of core tax logic

This governance model ensures legal accuracy while minimizing regulatory exposure.

Why This Framework Is Critical

Financial platforms demand the highest level of security and compliance. This framework protects users, reduces legal risk, and establishes long-term trust with regulators and stakeholders.
