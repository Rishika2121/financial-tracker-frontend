Admin Review & Validation Engine

A controlled compliance layer that validates tax data, deductions, and special provisions before final preparation.

Why Admin Review Exists

Shunyatax intentionally prevents direct tax finalization to reduce filing errors, audit risk, and incorrect claims.

  • Prevents unsupported or excessive deductions
  • Ensures correct section application
  • Identifies missing documents or inconsistencies
  • Adds professional compliance oversight

What Gets Reviewed

  • User identity & profile classification
  • Uploaded documents & proofs
  • Expense categorization accuracy
  • Eligibility engine results
  • Special cases (44AD, business income)

Review Outcomes

Validated & Approved

Data is complete, compliant, and approved for tax computation and filing readiness.

Flagged for Correction

Issues are identified and shared with the user for correction, clarification, or re-upload.

Purpose & Benefits

  • Additional compliance safety net
  • Lower audit and penalty exposure
  • Improved data quality
  • Higher confidence before filing

Regulatory Disclaimer

Admin review assists in compliance validation only. Shunyatax does not file returns or replace a licensed tax professional. Final filing responsibility remains with the user.
